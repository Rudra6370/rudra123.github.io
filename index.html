Why DoseMate?

Your Health Data Stays 100% Private

✓

Local-First Storage

All your health data is stored on your device using Hive & SharedPreferences. Nothing is sent to external servers.

✓

No Account Required

Start using DoseMate instantly. No sign-up, no cloud sync, no data breaches to worry about.

✓

Battery-Friendly Alarms

Uses Android Alarm Manager for reliable, exact alarms that won't drain your battery.

Backup & Restore

Export your data as a backup file and restore it anytime. You're always in control.

Your Data Never Leaves Your Device
