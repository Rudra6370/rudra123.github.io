DoseMate – Privacy Policy

Your health data stays on your device.

Effective Date: June 15, 2026
TL;DR: DoseMate stores all your health and medicine data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal health information. The only third-party service integrated is Google AdMob, which collects standard advertising identifiers as described below.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how DoseMate ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

By using DoseMate, you agree to the collection and use of information as outlined in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored entirely on your device using local storage (Hive & SharedPreferences). This data is never transmitted to our servers or any external server:

Data Type Examples Purpose
Medicine Reminders Medicine names, dosages, schedules, reminder times Schedule and manage medication reminders
Health Metrics Weight, height, BMI, water intake goals Calculate personalized health recommendations
Diet Plans Food entries, meal schedules, calorie tracking Track nutrition and diet plans
App Settings Theme preferences, notification settings, onboarding status Personalize your app experience
Usage History Dose taken/missed logs, streak data Track medication adherence
Important: Since all data is stored locally, clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App will permanently delete all your health information. We recommend keeping your own backup if needed.

2.2 Information Collected by Google AdMob

DoseMate uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Advertising ID – A unique, user-resettable identifier used for ad personalization.
  • Device Information – Device model, operating system version, language, screen resolution.
  • Network Information – IP address (used for approximate geolocation and fraud prevention), network type.
  • App Usage Data – Ad impressions, clicks, and interactions with ads.

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with the Google Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Information

We use the locally stored information solely to:

  • Send medication and water reminders via device alarms and notifications.
  • Calculate BMI-based water intake goals.
  • Track and display your medication adherence history and streaks.
  • Personalize the app experience (theme, settings).

We do not use your health data for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond the App's core functionality.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal health information with any third party.

The only third-party service integrated in the App is Google AdMob, which operates independently and is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and device information as described above.

We may disclose information only if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • All health data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted over the internet.
  • No personal health data is transmitted over the internet.
  • The App uses HTTPS for any network requests (AdMob only).

However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

DoseMate is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Since all data is on your device, you can access it at any time through the App.
  • Deletion: You can delete all your data by clearing the App's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the App.
  • Opt-out of ad personalization: You can reset your advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads in your device settings (Settings → Privacy → Ads).
